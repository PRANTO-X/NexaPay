Our Mission & Vision

At NexaPay, we believe that every business—regardless of size—should have access to the same payment infrastructure that powers the world's largest companies.

Founded in 2018, we set out to solve the complex challenges of payment processing that businesses face daily. Our vision is to create a world where financial transactions are seamless, secure, and accessible to everyone.

Our Journey

From a small startup to a global payment infrastructure provider

2018

Company Founded

NexaPay was founded by a team of payment industry veterans with a vision to simplify payment processing for businesses of all sizes.

2019

First Product Launch

Launched our core payment processing API, serving our first 100 customers and processing over $1M in transactions.

2020

Series A Funding & Global Expansion

Raised $15M in Series A funding and expanded our services to support 50+ countries and 100+ currencies.

2021

Advanced Fraud Protection

Introduced AI-powered fraud detection systems, reducing chargebacks by 75% for our customers.

2022

Enterprise Solutions & $50B Processed

Launched enterprise-grade solutions and surpassed $50B in annual payment volume processed.

2023

Today & Beyond

Serving 10,000+ businesses worldwide with plans to expand into new financial services and markets.
