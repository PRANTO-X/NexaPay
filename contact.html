Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

How quickly can I get started with NexaPay?

You can get started in minutes with our self-service onboarding. For enterprise customers with custom requirements, our team can help you get set up within 1-2 business days.

What payment methods do you support?

We support all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), digital wallets (Apple Pay, Google Pay), bank transfers, and local payment methods in over 150 countries.

Is my payment data secure with NexaPay?

Yes, security is our top priority. We are PCI DSS Level 1 certified, the highest level of certification in the payments industry. All sensitive data is encrypted and we never store full card numbers on our servers.

Do you offer custom pricing for high-volume businesses?

Yes, we offer custom pricing for businesses processing over $1M annually. Contact our sales team to discuss volume discounts and enterprise features tailored to your business needs.
